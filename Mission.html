Southfork field Services Mission Statement

Why Southfork Field Services is an industry leader in Excavating and continues to be a successful in Utah

Southfork is Companies are committed to long term enduring excellence through strategic alliances with our customers and providing our employees with a rewarding and safe work environment. To achieve this goal, we are continuously striving to work harder and smarter with more dedication and resolve each and every day, without ever compromising our integrity. These values are paramount to our continued success and reputation as an industry leader.

We are committed to providing our customers with cost effective construction services while ensuring the quality, schedule and budget goals meet or exceed their expectations, regardless of project size. We will encourage initiative and leadership of our employees while allowing for professional and personal growth, understanding that our employees are the resource that accomplishes our objectives.

Ultimately we have one vision, to become the company of choice for our customers and employees in helping build Utah for generations to come.
