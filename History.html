Southfork's History

Southfork Field Services, LLC was created in 2012, with a small oilfield contract. We have since grown to accommodate multiple projects simultaneously, including year-round oil and gas field maintenance, new construction, road work, pipeline installation and more. Our employees are dependable, efficient and professional, and our attention to detail is immeasurable. Southfork is proud to support the Uintah Basin's oil and gas industry.

Southfork has maintained the ability to survive the ups and downs of the oil and gas industry. Other businesses grow quickly and portray themselves as "big companies" in the industry...Southfork Field Services, LLC will only grow when needed in order to avoid unnecessary overhead, reductions in force, etc. The company will continue to seek stable contracts with long-term clients, but isn't afraid to take small projects that other businesses pass up. Southfork will remain flexible and willing to consider any project, regardless of size or scope!
